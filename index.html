About us

“Baba Azam” carries the essence and taste of the traditional roots with the high spirits of Authentic Biryani. We serve Biryani in the form of conventional love to keep its essence alive as Mumtaz Mahal introduced the Biryani Culture to the country, with its fondness and rich taste.
Baba Azam caters to that taste and enthusiasm of traditional Biryani that wakes from the hands of a mother who has been cooking Biryani just the right way for ages.
